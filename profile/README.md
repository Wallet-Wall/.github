# WalletWall

**Wallet intelligence for whale activity, stablecoin concentration, and post-quantum migration readiness.**

WalletWall explores how on-chain wallets behave, cluster, concentrate value, and expose risk over time. The project combines wallet analytics, graph-based discovery, stablecoin holder intelligence, and research into post-quantum wallet migration paths.

## Product surfaces

| Surface                  | Focus                                                                          |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Whale Watcher**        | Wallet-level holder intelligence, whale activity, and large-wallet behavior.   |
| **Stable Seer**          | Stablecoin holders, concentration, leaderboards, and distribution patterns.    |
| **Coinstellation**       | Token, pool, and wallet discovery through graph-based exploration.             |
| **Quantum Intelligence** | Wallet-level exposure, migration readiness, and post-quantum research framing. |

## Research direction

WalletWall is researching practical wallet migration paths for a post-quantum environment. The work focuses on clear user-facing risk language, wallet-level exposure modeling, verifier boundaries, and non-custodial migration-readiness patterns.

The research is not financial advice, not custody infrastructure, and not a claim that any specific wallet is compromised. It is a framework for understanding public wallet exposure and preparing safer migration paths.

## Security posture

WalletWall does **not** custody funds, request seed phrases, require private keys, or ask users to sign unsafe transactions.

Public research and prototypes are separated from production wallet-analysis surfaces. Experimental vault and verifier work should be treated as research unless explicitly marked otherwise.
